While there are ongoing efforts to build capacity in big data for SDGs in the UN through training and global inventories of standards and big data projects, there has yet to be a single platform to bring together both information about different data, and how it has been applied to SDG research and development. The SDG Data Hub brings together datasets, published methodology, and its impact on policy-making.

Learn more »

The SDG Data Hub brings together key indicator research projects, enables reproducible research and the sharing of methodology, consolidates training methodologies through interactive tutorials, and provides a focal point for governments, industry, research and the development communities broadly to explore and adopt open source data innovations.
